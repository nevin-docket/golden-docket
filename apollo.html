USE CASE 01

Apollo

Website Engagement & Visitor Conversion

What Apollo does

Apollo engages high-intent visitors on docketai.com in real time, answers buyer questions accurately, and guides them toward a next step — replacing the passive experience of a site that does nothing when visitors don't fill the form.

Apollo runs consultative BANT-style discovery to understand the visitor's use case, team, and pain. It distinguishes SMB, mid-market, and enterprise needs without sounding like a form, then routes to self-serve, a commercial rep, or the named account owner depending on segment and context.

What Apollo replaces

  • Passive websites where high-intent visitors leave without filling the form
  • Generic chatbots that surface FAQs but don't qualify
  • Slow lead-routing flows where SDRs follow up hours later

Key discovery questions

  • What happens today when a high-intent visitor lands on your site and doesn't fill the form?
  • What is your current website conversion rate (visitors to meetings or pipeline)?
  • How many of your monthly visitors would you consider high-intent?
