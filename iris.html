USE CASE 03

Iris

Inbound Automation (Post-Form Fill)

What Iris does

Many companies get form fills but lose leads between submission and SDR follow-up. Iris picks up immediately after the form, qualifies the lead in real time using frameworks like BANT or MEDDIC, and routes an Agent Qualified Lead (AQL) directly into the CRM — replacing the first 15-minute SDR discovery call.

Iris also reduces the spammy, unqualified meetings getting booked on salespeople's calendars. It recaps the form values, surfaces missed signals, and either flips to a meeting (Book a Meeting CTA) when warranted or routes to trial when intent is genuinely soft.

What Iris replaces

  • The 15-minute SDR discovery call that just re-asks the form questions
  • Lead-leakage between form submission and human follow-up
  • Calendar-clogging meetings with unqualified prospects

Key discovery questions

  • How many form fills are you getting per month, and how many get worked within the first hour?
  • What does your current SDR-to-inbound-lead ratio look like?
  • What qualification criteria do you use, and at what point in the funnel is it applied today?
