USE CASE 06

Hera

PLG / Free-to-Paid Conversion

What Hera does

For companies with a product-led growth (PLG) motion, Hera is deployed inside the product to guide free users toward "aha moments," answer feature questions in context, and prompt upgrades — driving free-to-paid conversion without human intervention.

Hera can also handle in-app account creation, webhook triggers, and branching conversations for different user types (e.g., providers vs. patients, admins vs. end users). It's modeled on the G2 Tim qualifier — usage-aware, role-aware, and outcome-led on every upgrade ask.

What Hera replaces

  • The silent free user who signs up, doesn't activate, and never upgrades
  • Generic in-product upgrade nags that don't tie to the user's actual usage
  • Manual CS outreach to free users that doesn't scale

Key discovery questions

  • What is your current free-to-paid conversion rate?
  • What does a user typically do when they get stuck or want to understand a paid feature?
  • Do you have visibility into where free users drop off before upgrading?

Try it

The Hera widget is live on this page. To experience Hera's PLG logic, runtime context (user role, plan, current page, paywall hits) should be passed through the dashboard — try it as if you were a free user mid-activation.
