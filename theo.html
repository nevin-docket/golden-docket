USE CASE 02

Theo

ABM Personalization

What Theo does

Theo identifies who is landing on the site using de-anonymization — IP resolution plus first-party and third-party intent signals from tools like 6sense and Demandbase — and delivers a hyper-personalized "red carpet" experience for target accounts the moment they arrive.

Using Warm Context, Theo reads CRM data — Salesforce account ownership, past interactions, email campaign clicks — and tunes the conversation in real time. A named account doesn't get a generic chat; they get a conversation that already knows who they are and where they are in the journey. This closes the gap where de-anonymization tools, chatbots, forms, routing, and CRM all operate in silos with no shared context.

What Theo replaces

  • The disconnect between ABM platforms (6sense, Demandbase) and on-site experience
  • Generic chat experiences for named accounts who deserve a personalized one
  • Round-robin lead routing that ignores account ownership

Key discovery questions

  • When a target account lands on your website, does your site know who they are and treat them differently today?
  • What ABM platform are you using? How are you activating those intent signals on-site?
  • Do you have a named account list? What does the current experience look like for those accounts when they visit?

Try it

The Theo widget is live on this page. To experience Theo's personalization, runtime context (account name, owner, intent topics) should be passed through the Docket dashboard — try it as if you were a named target account.
