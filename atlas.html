USE CASE 05

Atlas

Paid Ads Acceleration (Ad Landing Page Conversion)

What Atlas does

Companies spending $20K–$400K/month on Google, LinkedIn, or Bing campaigns lose value when the landing-page experience drops off after the click. High ad spend with low landing-page conversion is the signal.

Atlas is deployed on paid-ad landing pages to convert that expensive click into a qualified meeting in the same session — rather than losing the visitor to a static form or a dead-end page. Instead of sending ad clicks to a generic website, companies send them to an agent-powered landing page that engages, qualifies, and books immediately.

What Atlas replaces

  • Static landing pages with a single CTA and 1–3% conversion
  • Generic websites that don't know which campaign sent the visitor
  • The gap between ad click and form submission where most spend is wasted

Key discovery questions

  • What is your current monthly paid ads spend (campaign + agency)?
  • What is your landing-page conversion rate from paid clicks today?
  • Are you sending ad traffic to a dedicated landing page or to your main website?
